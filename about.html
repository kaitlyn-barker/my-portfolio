I'm an educator with a passion for using technology to enhance the learning experience. I believe in designing instruction that engages and meets the needs of every learner, and utilizing EdTech tools to their greatest potential.

Currently, I'm in the Master's of Educational Innovation, Technology, and Entrepreneurship at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. I'm particularly interested in AI, learning sciences, and XR experiences and love collaborating with others who share similar passions.

When I'm not studying, you can find me watching sports, reading, or cooking. I'm always eager to learn new things and take on exciting challenges.
